What is ValueStreak?

Have you ever looked at a videogame on eBay and wondered if it was a good deal?
Have you ever wanted to put your collection up for sale and wanted to know if you were getting a fair price for it?
ValueStreak is here to help.

ValueStreak is a new service that tells you what it's been selling for on eBay.
Just type the name of the game you're interested in buying or selling, select your platform of choice, and hit 'enter.'
ValueStreak will find up to the last 100 sales of that game on eBay and report the lowest, highest, and average sale prices, along with chart showing every individual sale to help you spot trends and avoid being fooled by outliers.
ValueStreak only reports completed sales, so you can rest assured that our results reflect prices that people are actually willing to pay.

If you buy or sell games on eBay, then you need ValueStreak.
